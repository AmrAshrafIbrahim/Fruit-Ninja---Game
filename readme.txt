Game Description
It is a single player game in which the player slices fruits with a blade controlled by mouse
swipes (finger swipes on touch screen in case of smartphones). As the fruits are thrown onto
the screen, the player swipes the mouse across the screen to create a slicing motion,
attempting to slice the fruit into two halves.
Rules and Tasks
● You have at least the following:
o 3 types of Fruits .
o 2 types of Bombs:
▪ Fatal : ends the game immediately.
▪ Dangerous : makes you lose one life.
o 2 types of Special Fruits that add special extra credit points upon slicing.
● Your goal is to beat the highest score
